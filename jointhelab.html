Join the lab

We are a dynamic and collaborative research team focused on understanding epithelial development. We are always looking for talented and motivated individuals to join our group. If you are passionate about development, cell biology, genetics or high-resolution confocal imaging to CRISPR-based genome editing, we encourage you to apply!


Who Can Join

  • Integrated PhD/PhD Students: For information on Integrated PhD/PhD positions or how to apply, please visit TIFR Graduate Studies Page.
  • Postdoctoral Researchers: Highly motivated and independent postdoctoral researchers are encouraged to apply. Please send your CV, cover letter, and the names of three references directly to mahendras@tifr.res.in.
  • Project Interns: We offer project internship opportunities for undergraduate and graduate students. Interested candidates should send their CV and a brief statement of interest to mahendras@tifr.res.in.

Current Openings:

Keep an eye on this page for announcements of open positions. We regularly update this section with new opportunities as they become available.
